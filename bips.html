What Are BIPs

A new kind of international learning experience.

Blended Intensive Programmes are short-term Erasmus+ courses that mix a physical mobility period abroad with a virtual component — letting you experience an international campus in just a few weeks.

⏱️

Short & Focused

5–30 day on-site programmes combined with online modules — designed to fit around your studies without a full semester commitment.

🌐

Multicultural Teams

Work shoulder-to-shoulder with students from at least three European countries on real-world, interdisciplinary challenges.

💡

Innovative Learning

Project-based labs, design thinking sprints, and field visits — powered by a blend of digital tools and face-to-face collaboration.

Why Join a BIP

Compact programmes. Lifelong impact.

Everything a full semester abroad can teach you — condensed into an intensive, project-driven, and fully-funded format.

🌍

International Experience

Live, learn and collaborate in a European city alongside students and professors from across the continent.

⚡

Short & Intensive

Maximum learning in minimum time — a few focused weeks that fit around your existing degree.

🎓

Academic Credits (ECTS)

Every BIP grants between 3 and 9 ECTS credits, fully recognised by your home institution.

🤝

Networking Opportunities

Join a pan-European alumni network of students, researchers and industry partners that lasts beyond the programme.
